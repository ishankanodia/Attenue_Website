Welcome to Attenue!

Revolutionize your classroom experience with our cutting-edge attendance software. Attenue captures classroom images to automatically register student attendance, ensuring a smooth and non-intrusive process that saves valuable time for professors and students alike.

About Us

Our Vision: At Attenue, we believe in leveraging technology to enhance education. Our software is designed to optimize the attendance process, reducing the manual effort involved and allowing educators to focus on what matters most: teaching.

What is Attenue? Attenue is an advanced software solution that utilizes image recognition technology to take attendance effortlessly. By capturing images of the classroom, Attenue automatically records the presence of students without interrupting the flow of the class.

Features

  • Non-Intrusive: Attenue operates seamlessly in the background, ensuring that classroom activities are not disturbed.
  • Time-Saving: No more roll calls or manual attendance marking. Professors can save precious time and focus on delivering quality education.
  • Accurate & Reliable: Attenue ensures that every student's attendance is recorded with precision, reducing errors and discrepancies.
  • Easy Integration: Our software can be easily integrated into existing systems, making it a convenient choice for educational institutions.

How It Works

  1. Classroom Setup: Install Attenue's camera in your classroom. The camera captures images at scheduled intervals.
  2. Image Processing: Our software processes the captured images and identifies each student using advanced image recognition algorithms.
  3. Attendance Marking: The attendance is the automatically recorded and saved in the system, ready for professors to review.
